# Hackathon Info

## 심사 기준 (Judging Criteria)

### Judge 심사 (80점)

| Category | Score | What We're Looking For |
|----------|-------|----------------------|
| Technical Execution | 20 | Depth and appropriateness of NeMo stack usage, code quality, architecture, working demo |
| Innovation | 15 | Novelty and creativity of the solution, differentiation from existing approaches |
| Impact | 20 | Real-world usefulness, scalability, and business relevance |
| Presentation | 10 | Clarity of pitch, ability to articulate technical choices and trade-offs |
| Track-specific Completeness | 15 | Track A: Completeness of the system / Track B: Completeness of overall training process, and the validity of the metrics / Track C: Completeness of the dataset to the point that it is usable |

### Peer Review (20점)

| Category | Score | Detail |
|----------|-------|--------|
| Peer-review | 20 | In each track, teams can vote for one team, and teams will be awarded points in descending order based on the number of votes received (20, 18, 16, 14, 12, 10, 8), only in tracks in which they are not participating |

> **Final score** = average across all judges. Projects must include at least one NeMo Framework component or Microservice, a reproducible codebase, and a working demo to be eligible.
